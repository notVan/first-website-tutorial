How many services do we have?

Currently, the church runs one service in the morning.

When does church close?

Service starts at 9:00AM and ends at 11:30AM.
We highly suggest you join a ministry to keep you rooted.

When do ministries meet?

Right after church at the various ministry meeting points.

Do I get to speak to the Bishop easily?

Our Bishop is full of love and care, he would like nothing more than
to get to know you.

Is there a children's service?

Yes! Your children are taken good care of and are fed with great
knowledge of the word of God by pastors that care about each of them.
